Paid Android app

Standalone list management without the subscription drag.

Local List Assist for Android is the standalone version of the product. It is priced at $0.99, stores data locally, and avoids the ad-driven or account-heavy patterns that make everyday utility apps worse over time.

Included in version 1

  • Multiple lists
  • Grocery and flat list templates
  • Category-aware grocery grouping
  • Duplicate handling
  • Completed history
  • Archived lists
  • Recent activity
  • Local backup and restore

Product stance

This is a paid utility app, not a free app with hidden monetization pressure. The current launch direction is simple: no ads, no analytics, no account requirement, and no cloud dependency.

Availability

The Android release is being prepared for Google Play. If you want launch access or updates, email support@locallistassist.app.
